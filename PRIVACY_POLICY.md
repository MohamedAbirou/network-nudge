## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate information
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Opt out of marketing and analytics

**How to Exercise**: Email privacy@networknudge.com with your request.

### 6.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

- **Knowledge**: Know what personal information we collect and use
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of collected data
- **Opt-Out**: Opt out of the "sale" of personal information (we don't sell, but you can opt-out of data sharing)
- **Non-Discrimination**: We won't discriminate for exercising your rights

**How to Exercise**: Submit a request at [privacy portal link] or email privacy@networknudge.com.

### 6.3 Other Rights

- **Unsubscribe**: Click the link in any marketing email
- **Cookie Opt-Out**: Disable cookies in your browser settings
- **Analytics Opt-Out**: Opt out of Google Analytics via browser extension

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures

- **Encryption**: LinkedIn tokens encrypted at rest using libsodium
- **HTTPS**: All data in transit encrypted with TLS 1.2+
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0 for LinkedIn, password hashing with bcrypt
- **Database**: Supabase Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
- **Access Control**: Only authenticated users access their own data
- **Audit Logs**: All data access logged for monitoring

### 7.2 Limitations

No security is perfect. We cannot guarantee 100% security. Report vulnerabilities to security@networknudge.com (see our Security Policy).
